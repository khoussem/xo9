How to play XO9



  		•















    Win A Cell

    Red Must Play Top Right

    When the game starts, the first player can play anywhere. Once the first player plays, the second player must play on the board of the cell that the first player chose. If the board that the first player chose is already won, the second player may play anywhere.



  		•















    Win A Board

    Blue Wins Top Right Board with Middle Column

    Win a board like winning a classic game of Tic-Tac-Toe. All you need to do to win the board is to win three in a row horizontally, vertically or diagonally. If either player wins a board, the board will be invalid for play for the rest of the game. Any player who pics a cell on another board that corresponds to the won board will give the next player an opportunity to play in any board they chose.

Why play XO9?

Features

Why play XO9?

Multiple Play Modes

Three Play Modes

With XO9, you get three play modes to challenge yourself and your friends. Play versus the computer if you're offline or just want to practice your XO9 skills. Challenge a friend sharing the same phone or challenge a friend using Game Center over the network.

iPhone and iPad Ready

User Interface for iPhone and iPad

XO9 is designed to run on your iPad, iPod, and iPhone. Each user interface is designed to to provide you with the highest resolution for your gaming experience, by taking advantage of screen real estate. XO9 also uses retina graphics give you a great gaming experience on your retina device.

Robots

Sharpen Your Skills Against Bots

Learn the game or perfect your XO9 strategy by playing against one of three bots.

Dumbot

The easiest bot and is a great opponent for kids and learning the game.

Cleverbot

More difficult and uses strategy in determining where to play next.

Geniusbot

For experts and uses a precision strategy to select it's next mov.

Multiple Themes

Theme Your Game The Way You Want

XO9 comes with one theme and five upgradable themes. Memorial Day comes free, but you can upgrade to get the rest of the themes for customizing your game.
