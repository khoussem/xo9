Rules

How to play XO9

  • 9 Boards

    The Tame Has 9 Game Boards

    XO9 is comprised of 9 Game Boards. The boards are like a regular board in the game of Tic-Tac-Toe.

  		•

    9 Cells

    Each Board Has 9 Cells

    Within each board, there are 9 individual cells, like a mini Tic-Tac-Toe game.

  		•

    Win A Cell

    Win The Cell In A Board

    The Player X plays a cell in one of the boards. Player O must play in the board that corresponds to the cell that Player X played in.

  		•

    Set Next Board

    Play In The Highlighted Board

    Player O plays a cell in the highlighted board set by Player X. Once Player O plays a cell, Player X must play in the board that corresponds to the cell Player O played in.

  		•

    Win A Board

    Blue Wins Top Right Board

    Win a board like winning a classic game of Tic-Tac-Toe. All you need to do to win the board is to win three in a row horizontally, vertically or diagonally. If either player wins a board, the board will be invalid for play for the rest of the game. A player who plays a cell which corresponds to a board that is won gives the next player an opportunity to play any cell on any board.

  		•

    Win The Game

    Red Wins Game with Left Column

    Winning three boards in a row wins you the game. Red won the game by winning the column on the left

Features

Why play XO9?

Features

Why play XO9?

Multiple Play Modes

Three Play Modes

With XO9, you get three play modes to challenge yourself and your friends. Play versus the computer if you're offline or just want to practice your XO9 skills. Challenge a friend sharing the same phone or challenge a friend using Game Center over the network.

iPhone and iPad Ready

User Interface for iPhone and iPad

XO9 is designed to run on your iPad, iPod, and iPhone. Each user interface is designed to to provide you with the highest resolution for your gaming experience, by taking advantage of screen real estate. XO9 also uses retina graphics give you a great gaming experience on your retina device.

Press play to listen to the bots

Robots

Sharpen Your Skills Against Bots

Learn the game or perfect your XO9 strategy by playing against one of three bots.

Dumbot
The easiest bot and is a great opponent for kids and learning the game.
Cleverbot
More difficult and uses strategy in determining where to play next.
Geniusbot
Expert difficulty using precision strategy to select its next move.

Challenges

Unlock achievments and climb the leaderboard

XO9 has lots of achievements in the game designed to test your skill. Achievements range from basic rewards scuh as winning the game to strategic rewards like beating an opponent and not letting them win a square

Multiple Themes

Theme Your Game The Way You Want

XO9 comes with one theme but is upgradable to five additional themes. Memorial Day comes free, but you can upgrade to get Spring Break, Halloween, New Years Eve, July 4th, and Thanksgiving.
